Background

I am a senior at Northeastern University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. While studying at Northeastern University, I had the opportunity to participate in the Co-op program working at three companies full-time for over two and a half years. My first experience as a Co-op took me to Cambridge, Massachusetts, where I worked as a Software Modeling, Analysis and Validation Co-op at Draper Laboratory, testing software and co-founding a software test automation team. My second experience brought me to Imprivata, a healthcare software security company in Lexington, Massachusetts, where I worked as a Software Engineering Co-op developing healthcare applications. Lastly, my third experience was at Amazon Robotics in North Reading, Massachusetts, where I helped the Systems Engineering team drive forward new cutting-edge technology for Amazon Fulfillment Centers across the world.
